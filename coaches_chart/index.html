Percentage of nonwhite Divison I men's basketball coaches in six largest conferences
Diversity is down in the Big Ten and the five other major men's basketball conferences. Explore the trends for each conference over the years. Additionally, the diversity gap between nonwhite coaches and their nonwhite players has been increasing since the early 2000s. For example, in the Big Ten in 2000, the percentage of nonwhite players was about twice the percentage of nonwhite coaches. In 2015, the percentage of nonwhite players was more than triple the percentage of coaches.
