Minnesota tries to sell land
A decade ago, the Legislature ordered the DNR to start auctioning off unneeded parcels of public land – both to raise revenue and to clean up the DNR’s real estate holdings. Sales have been slow and bids have been low. Maps closed sales of state land by the DNR per county, spanning the years 2007 to 2017. Shaded from less to more.
