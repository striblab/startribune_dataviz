Upgrading Minnesota’s Public Lands
The DNR has sold state-owned land for most of its history. Now it’s working to upgrade the state’s holdings by pruning scattered, low-priority parcels for money to buy better public land. Here’s a map of land sold over the last decade at the DNR’s public auctions and over the counter. Public sales made up about 60 percent of the DNR’s total land sales during the decade.
