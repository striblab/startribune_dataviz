See the reduction of deficient bridges in the past 10 years
Structurally deficient bridges are those designated by federal guidelines as having significant deterioration of its deck, supports or other major components that need oversight. The number of such bridges dropped dramatically in Minnesota between 2006 and 2017. Use the slider to swipe between years to see the different. Use the zoom controls to see more detail.
