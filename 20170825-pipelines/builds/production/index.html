Minnesota pipeline incidents by year
Since 2002, about 125 hazardous liquid spills in Minnesota have resulted in two deaths, two injuries and about $40 million in damage.

Enbridge pipeline incidents in Minnesota by year
About 60 hazardous liquid spills have occurred on Enbridge-operated pipelines in Minnesota since 2002, a third of which were considered significant, meaning 210 more gallons in volume were spilled, or the incident resulted in an explosion, death, injury or $50,000 in damage.
Source: PHMSA, Star Tribune analysis | Graphic by Jeff Hargarten, Star Tribune | Download Data
Pipelines spills in Minnesota by gallons
Since 2002, there have been about 336,300 gallons spilled on Enbridge pipelines -- mostly of crude oil -- in Minnesota, about a third of the more than 1 million gallons of hazardous liquids released statewide over the time period.
