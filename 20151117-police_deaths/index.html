Fatal police encounters

Fatal police encounters

The Star Tribune collected the names and stories of everyone who died after a physical confrontation with law enforcement in Minnesota from 2000 through May 2016. A disproportionate share — at least 45 percent — were people with a history of mental illness or who were having a mental health crisis when they died. 
By Jeff Hargarten, Jennifer Bjorhus, MaryJo Webster, and Kelly Smith Star Tribune
